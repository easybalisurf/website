3. Payments

Unless stated otherwise at confirmation, payment for sessions, packages and add-ons is made directly to the Provider (cash, card or transfer on the day, or via a payment link we may send on the Provider's behalf). easybali.surf may charge a separate, clearly disclosed booking or platform fee where applicable.

Reschedules and cancellations are handled with the Provider directly, subject to that Provider's own cancellation policy, which will be shared at confirmation. Bali's weather and ocean conditions can change quickly — Providers may propose a different time, spot or date for safety reasons, at no extra cost to you.
